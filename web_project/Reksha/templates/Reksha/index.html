ABOUT US

As part of the rescue forces, we perform critical functions to save lives and reduce harm during emergencies.We collaborate with various other agencies and brings an effective result on this field. We work to remove individuals from dangerous situations, like collapsed buildings or disaster zones, using specialized tools and techniques. Ensuring the safe evacuation of people from hazardous areas is a priority, especially for vulnerable groups like children, the elderly, those with disabilities, and ladies, guiding them to secure locations. As a first responder, we provide immediate medical assistance—whether it's administering first aid, performing CPR, or offering advanced care—to stabilize victims before they receive further treatment. In the aftermath of disasters, We focus on recovery operations, which involve retrieving and preserving property, remains, or hazardous materials, helping to bring closure and facilitate recovery efforts. Effective coordination and communication are key to what we do, collaborating with various agencies, establishing command centers, and sharing real-time updates to ensure resources are deployed efficiently. Each of these functions allows us to respond swiftly and effectively, making a tangible difference for those affected by crises.
